PARKME is a system designed to help users find available car parks in their preferred location and to take the stress out of finding a carpark.
ParkMe aims to save users valuable time by cutting out the time wasted while driving around in circles searching for a place to park

Whether you are a new driver, experienced driver, passenger or co-pilot, ParkMe is designed to be used byeveryone. With an easy to use interface and voice automated directions, ParkMe can be used to facilitate the search for a car park in any location.

Using real-time tracking technologies to guide users to an available carpark, users will be able to search for parks in any location they choose, sorting the results by proximity, the cost of the car park or the parks time limit before choosing their preferred park and receiveing directions with the choice of voice automated guidance.
